11 """
12 Quadratic(bc::BoundaryCondition)
13
14 Indicate that the corresponding axis should use quadratic interpolation.
15
16 # Extended help
17
18 Assuming uniform knots with spacing 1, the `i`th piece of quadratic spline
19 implemented here is defined as follows:
20
21 y_i(x) = cm p(x-i) + c q(x) + cp p(1-(x-i))
22
23 where
24
25 p(δx) = (δx - 1)^2 / 2
26 q(δx) = 3/4 - δx^2
27
28 and the values for `cX` for `X ∈ {m,_,p}` are the pre-filtered coefficients.
29
30 For future reference, this expands to the following polynomial:
31
32 y_i(x) = cm * 1/2 * (x-i-1)^2 + c * (3/4 - x + i)^2 + cp * 1/2 * (x-i)^2
33
34 When we derive boundary conditions we will use derivatives `y_1'(x-1)` and
35 `y_1''(x-1)`
36 """
